Synopsis

Big Buck Bunny" is a charming animated short film that follows the story of a gentle and oversized rabbit named Big Buck Bunny. Living peacefully in his lush meadow, Big Buck Bunny's tranquil existence is disrupted when a trio of mischievous rodents decides to torment him for their own amusement. Unwilling to tolerate their bullying behavior, Big Buck Bunny devises a clever plan to turn the tables on his tormentors and teach them a lesson they won't soon forget. Filled with heartwarming moments, stunning animation, and a delightful sense of humor, "Big Buck Bunny" is a captivating tale that celebrates the triumph of kindness and ingenuity over adversity
